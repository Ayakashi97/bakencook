# User Guide

## Dashboard
The dashboard provides an overview of your recent activities, upcoming baking schedules, and quick access to your favorite recipes.

## Recipes
- **Browse**: View all your recipes in a grid or list view.
- **Import**: Import recipes from supported websites (e.g., Chefkoch, Ploetzblog) by pasting the URL. The AI will automatically parse ingredients and steps.
- **Create/Edit**: Manually create recipes or edit imported ones. You can adjust ingredients, steps, and add images.
- **Scale**: Automatically scale ingredient quantities based on the desired number of portions or yield.

## Planner
- **Schedule**: Plan your baking or cooking sessions.
- **Timeline**: View a timeline of steps for your scheduled recipes. The planner accounts for fermentation times and preparation steps.

## Shopping List
- **Generate**: Create a shopping list based on your planned recipes for a specific date range.
- **Manage**: Add custom items, check off purchased items, and clear the list.

## Profile
- **Settings**: Update your password and manage personal preferences.
